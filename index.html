Products

Stretch Film

Flexible plastic material that is commonly used to wrap and secure items together. It's often used for packaging and shipping purposes.

Plastic Bag

Is a lightweight, flexible bag made of thin plastic material. It typically has an opening at the top that can be sealed or tied, and it's commonly used for carrying groceries, goods, or other items.

Volatile Corrosion Inhibitor (VCI) Plastic Bag

is a special kind of bag made from plastic that contains materials designed to protect metal items from corrosion.

Ziplock Bag

is a type of plastic bag that has a special zipper-like seal on the top. This seal allows you to easily open and close the bag by sliding the zipper mechanism.

Bubble Bag

is a type of packaging that has a layer of bubble wrap attached to it. The bubble wrap consists of small air-filled bubbles that create a cushioning effect.

PE foam bag

is a type of packaging that includes a layer of foam made from polyethylene (PE), a type of plastic. This foam provides cushioning and protection for items placed inside the bag.
